Crystal Collector!


    Instructions on how to play game

    You will be given a random number to start the game.

    There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

    You win the game by matching your score to the random number

    You will lose the game if your score goes above the random number.

    The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

    Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
